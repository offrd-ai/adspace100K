About Dubuque Radon

Locally focused, certified, and accountable radon professionals.

We're a locally focused radon testing and mitigation company serving Dubuque and the Tri-State area — including Asbury, Peosta, Sageville, Key West. Radon isn't a national franchise problem to us; it's a local one. The geology here drives some of the highest residential radon readings in the country, and we've built our whole business around fixing it for our neighbors.

Certified, guaranteed, accountable

Every system follows AARST/ANSI consensus standards, and every mitigation job ends with a post-installation test so you have documented proof your home dropped below 4.0 pCi/L. If a system we install doesn't get you there, we make it right — that's what the 5-year warranty is for.

Straightforward from the first call

Radon work shouldn't come with a sales pitch. We test honestly, quote a flat price, and explain exactly what your home needs and why. If your levels are fine, we'll tell you. Call (563) 213-0199 and you'll talk to someone local who does this every day.
