Serving Dubuque and Dubuque County. The right engineering makes a system effective, quiet, and easy to forget.

Systems designed for your foundation

  • Full basement — standard active sub-slab depressurization.
  • Crawl space — sub-membrane depressurization with a sealed vapor barrier.
  • Sump pit homes — sealed sump lids integrated into the system.
  • Drain-tile systems — tie-ins that use existing drainage as the suction network.
  • Mixed / multi-level foundations — multiple suction points balanced into one system.

Quiet, clean, code-compliant

A good install is one you forget is there. We size the fan to move the right volume of air without droning, route piping along logical paths, and finish exterior work to look intentional. Every installation meets AARST/ANSI standards and local code, with proper electrical, labeling, and a system monitor.

The system monitor matters

Every system includes a U-tube manometer — the gauge that shows your fan is maintaining suction. We show you how to read it in five seconds, so you'll know instantly if it ever needs service. Combined with our 5-year warranty, your protection is ongoing.

Repairs & second opinions

Inherited a loud system, or a reading that's still high after someone else's install? We troubleshoot and repair existing systems too. Call (563) 213-0199 for an honest second opinion.
