Radon Testing in Sioux City, IA

The only way to know your home is safe — and in Sioux City, the odds make testing essential.

📞 Call (712) 226-0199
Iowa leads the nation with 71.6% of homes above the EPA action level of 4.0 pCi/L, and Woodbury County sits in the highest-risk EPA Radon Zone 1.

We provide certified, accurate radon testing for homeowners, buyers, sellers, and businesses across Woodbury County. Because radon is invisible and odorless, professional testing is a genuine health screening for your household — not a formality.

Short-term testing

A short-term test runs 48 hours to about 7 days — the fastest way to a reading and the standard during real estate transactions. We use continuous radon monitors under closed-house conditions so the result reflects a true worst-case snapshot, not a lucky low reading.

Long-term testing

Radon swings with the seasons, peaking in winter when your home is sealed against the cold. A long-term test of 90+ days gives the most accurate year-round average — the gold standard when a short-term result lands near the action level.

  • An honest recommendation — if you don't need mitigation, we'll say so.
